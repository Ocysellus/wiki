Epistemology is the study of knowledge.

1 Hypothesis

A hypothesis is a proposed explanation or prediction method for a phenomenon. Only statistically tested hypothesis may become common knowledge.

Epicurus’ principle of multiple explanations. If multiple hypotheses are consistent with the observed data, one should retain them all.

Occam’s razor. Among all hypotheses consistent with the observations, choose the simplest.
